About Us

Robert Pederson

Robert Pederson founded the Edmonds Makerspace with a vision to cultivate a vibrant and innovative community hub. Motivated by a deep passion for creativity and hands-on learning, Pederson recognized the transformative power of a space dedicated to making, tinkering, and collaboration. His goal was to provide a welcoming environment where individuals could explore their creative potential and bring their ideas to life. The Edmonds Makerspace emerged as a haven for diverse minds eager to engage in a wide spectrum of projects, from woodworking and electronics to art and technology. By establishing the Edmonds Makerspace, Robert Pederson aimed to build a community that celebrates the joy of creation and encourages lifelong learning, ultimately contributing to the growth of a dynamic and inventive society.
