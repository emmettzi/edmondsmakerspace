Welcome to Our Company

Edmonds Makerspace, where creativity meets community. Created with the passion for creation, we provide a community for makers of all ages and backgrounds. Our mission is to inspire, educate and empower the people of our community with the access of cutting-edge tools and a network of professionals. At Edmonds Makerspace we believe in the power of making, and we invite you to join us on a journey of skill building.
